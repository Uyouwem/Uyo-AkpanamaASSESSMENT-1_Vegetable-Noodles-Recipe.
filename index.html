Noodles with vegetable Omelet

A vibrant, elevated take on the classic instant noodle dish, packed with fresh vegetables and a protein-rich side.

Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 10 mins

Servings: 1 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Indomie Belleful
  • 2 large eggs (for the omelet)
  • 1/4 cup diced fresh carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped green beans
  • 1/2 small red onion, sliced
  • 2 fresh bird's eye chilies (for heat)

Instructions

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil and cook the noodles for 3 minutes.
  2. Whisk eggs with a pinch of salt and fry into a thin, tightly rolled omelet.
  3. Drain the noodles thoroughly, leaving no excess water.
  4. Stir in the provided seasoning oils and powders until the noodles are glossy.
  5. Sauté the carrots and beans quickly and toss them into the noodle mix.
  6. Plate the noodles and garnish with raw onions and sliced chilies.

Chef's Tips

Note: Do not overcook the noodles! Keeping them "al dente" prevents them from becoming mushy when mixed with the hot veggies.
